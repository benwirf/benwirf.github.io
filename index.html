Conservation Seed Banking in the NT

A collection of information and data from the George Brown Darwin Botanic Gardens conservation seed bank

Search Partners Links Contact

Since 2015 George Brown Darwin Botanic Gardens Seed Bank has been working to support biodiversity conservation in the Northern Territory through securing seed collections of native flora in long term conservation storage.

The seed bank holds:

  • Around 460 collections in long term conservation storage
  • 360 species represented
  • 78 species of conservation significance* including:

  • 28 Data Deficient
  • 40 Near Threatened
  • 8 Vulnerable
  • 1 Endangered
  • 1 Critically Endangered
  • 7 EPBC** Listed species

* Listed under the Territory Parks & Wildlife Conservation Act
** Environmental Protection & Biodiversity Conservation Act
